Easy PanCake🥞 Recipe !!

Pancakes are simply just flat cakes made from a thin batter fried on both sides on griddle or frying pan. I really love🥰 pancakes because they are easy to make, you only need a few ingredients and a delectable😋 treat on a lazy Saturday morning after an arduous week.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Preparation:(5 minutes)

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt🧂 .
  2. Make a well in the center, and pour in oil,egg🥚 and milk🥛 .
  3. Mix until smooth.

Cooking: (10 minutes)

  1. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat♨ .
  2. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake🥞 .
  3. Brown on both sides and serve hot. Enjoy 😋
